Safety & Handling Guidelines

Proper handling ensures best results and personal safety

Personal Protection

  • Always wear nitrile gloves
  • Use safety glasses
  • Ensure good ventilation
  • Avoid skin contact

Storage Conditions

  • Store in dark, cool place
  • Temperature: 15-25°C
  • Keep containers sealed
  • Avoid UV exposure

Disposal Guidelines

  • Cure waste resin before disposal
  • Follow local regulations
  • Use UV light for curing
  • Recycle containers when possible
